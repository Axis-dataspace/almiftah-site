Admissions

We welcome applications for all year groups throughout the year. Our admissions team is here to guide you every step of the way.

How to Apply

A Simple, Four-Step Process

Joining Al-Miftah School is straightforward. Follow these four steps and our admissions team will support you every step of the way.

1

Submit an Enquiry

Complete the enquiry form below or contact our admissions office directly by phone or email. Tell us about your child, their current year group, and your preferred start date.

2

Book a Campus Visit

We warmly invite all prospective families to visit our campus, meet our leadership team, and experience our vibrant school community firsthand. We run open days throughout the year.

3

Submit Your Application

Complete the official application form and submit all required documents. For Secondary and Sixth Form applicants, a brief assessment may be arranged at this stage.

4

Receive Your Offer

Our admissions team will review your application and respond within 5 business days. Upon accepting an offer, a registration fee secures your child's place.

What You'll Need

Admission Requirements

Requirements vary by school section. Please prepare the documents relevant to your child's year group.

Early Years / Nursery

  • Completed application form
  • Child's birth certificate
  • Immunisation records
  • Passport-size photos (x2)
  • Parent/guardian ID

Primary School

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school reports (last 2 years)
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Reference from current school
  • Short assessment (English & Maths)

Secondary School

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school reports (last 2 years)
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Confidential reference
  • Entrance assessment

Sixth Form Entry

  • Completed application form
  • IGCSE / O-Level results or predicted grades
  • School reference letter
  • Personal statement
  • Interview with Head of Sixth Form

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept mid-year admissions?
Yes, we welcome applications throughout the academic year, subject to availability in each year group. Please contact our admissions office to check current availability.
Is there an entrance exam?
For Early Years and Lower Primary, no formal exam is required — we focus on a settling visit and informal observation. For Upper Primary, Secondary, and Sixth Form, a short assessment in English and Mathematics is arranged to ensure we can best support your child.
Do you offer scholarships or bursaries?
Yes, Al-Miftah School offers a limited number of academic scholarships and means-tested bursaries each year. Please contact our admissions office for details and eligibility criteria.
What language of instruction does the school use?
The primary language of instruction at Al-Miftah is English. We also offer Swahili and Arabic as core languages, and provide English as an Additional Language (EAL) support for students who require it.
When is the next Open Day?
Our next Open Day is on 20 June 2026 from 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM. We also offer private guided tours on request throughout the term. Contact us to book your visit.
How long does the admissions process take?
Once we receive a completed application with all required documents, we aim to respond within 5 business days. For assessment-based applications, the process typically takes 1–2 weeks from submission to offer.

About Al-Miftah School

A Community Built on Excellence & Values

Al-Miftah School is a co-educational institution serving students from Nursery through Sixth Form, offering a rigorous and internationally recognised curriculum in a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Our dedicated educators inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and a love of learning. We believe every child has unique potential — and our role is to help them discover and develop it within a community rooted in respect, integrity, and ambition.

Cambridge International Curriculum
Small class sizes for personalised learning
Modern facilities & technology-rich classrooms
Vibrant co-curricular & sports programmes

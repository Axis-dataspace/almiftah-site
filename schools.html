Our Four Schools

An outstanding educational journey from Nursery through Sixth Form — designed to meet every learner exactly where they are.

Early Years Primary Secondary Sixth Form
Early Years
Early Years / Nursery
Ages 3 – 5

Where Learning Begins with Joy

Our Early Years provision offers a warm, stimulating and beautifully resourced environment where our youngest learners can explore, discover, and build the essential foundations for lifelong learning through play-based inquiry and guided discovery.

Each child is seen as an individual with unique strengths and interests. Our experienced Early Years practitioners nurture every child's social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development — ensuring they are confident and ready for Primary school.

EYFS Framework Play-Based Learning Bilingual Support Outdoor Learning Creative Arts Phonics & Early Literacy
Low adult-to-child ratios for personalised attention
Purpose-built Early Years classrooms & outdoor play areas
Daily communication with parents via our app
Nurturing, inclusive environment for all children

Primary School
Ages 6 – 11 (Years 1 – 6)

Building Confident, Curious Learners

Our Primary School follows the Cambridge Primary Programme, providing a robust, internationally benchmarked curriculum that nurtures academic excellence alongside creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking skills.

Students develop a strong grounding in core subjects whilst exploring a rich co-curricular programme including sports, performing arts, digital literacy, and STEM activities that bring learning to life beyond the classroom.

Cambridge Primary English & Mathematics Sciences ICT & Computing STEM Projects Swahili & Arabic
Cambridge Primary Checkpoint assessments
Well-equipped classrooms with interactive whiteboards
Dedicated library and reading programme
Extensive sports and arts enrichment programme

Secondary School
Secondary School
Ages 12 – 16 (Years 7 – 11)

Developing Independent Thinkers

Our Secondary School provides a challenging and engaging curriculum through Cambridge Lower Secondary (Years 7–9) and Cambridge IGCSE (Years 10–11) programmes, equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and qualifications to thrive in Sixth Form and beyond.

Students choose from a wide range of subjects at IGCSE, supported by outstanding teachers, well-equipped laboratories, and a strong emphasis on leadership development and university preparation.

Cambridge IGCSE Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) Humanities Languages Business Studies Computer Science
Wide range of IGCSE subject choices
Fully equipped science and computer laboratories
Student leadership programme and prefect system
Early university and career guidance from Year 9
Enquire About Secondary Admissions
Sixth Form
Ages 17 – 18 (Years 12 – 13)

Your Gateway to the World's Top Universities

Al-Miftah Sixth Form offers A-Level and BTEC programmes across a broad range of subjects, combined with comprehensive UCAS and international university application support. Our graduates consistently secure places at top universities across the UK, USA, and East Africa.

Sixth Form students benefit from specialist teaching, small class sizes, an Extended Project Qualification (EPQ), and a rich enrichment programme designed to develop well-rounded, ambitious young adults.

A-Level Programmes BTEC Qualifications Extended Project (EPQ) UCAS Support SAT/ACT Preparation Scholarship Guidance
100% university placement rate for our graduates
Dedicated university counsellors for each student
Study rooms, common room & sixth form centre
Leadership roles and community service programme
